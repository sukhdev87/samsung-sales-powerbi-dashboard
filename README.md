# 📊 Samsung Product & Sales Analytics — Power BI Dashboard

## Overview
A multi-page Power BI dashboard analyzing Samsung's sales data across 
products, customer behavior, and global markets. Built as part of a 
Data Analyst portfolio to demonstrate real-world business intelligence skills.

---

## 📁 Dashboard Pages

### 1. Executive Sales Overview
- Total Revenue: $22.9M | Units Sold: 36,580
- Net Revenue trends across 2021–2024
- Top 10 countries by Revenue & Units Sold

### 2. Product Performance Analysis
- Top 10 Best-Selling Products: Price vs Discount
- Category-wise Gross/Net Revenue, Return Rate
- Units Sold by Storage Variant

### 3. Customer Behavior & Market Insights
- Sales by Age Group, Channel, and Payment Method
- Customer Segment discount analysis
- Previous Device OS conversion tracking

---

## 🔍 Key Business Insights
- Smart TV drives highest revenue despite low volume (premium pricing)
- Galaxy Z has highest return rate (0.11) — foldable fragility issue
- Business & Govt segments buy at lowest discount — strong brand trust
- iOS users hardest to convert (only 2.6K)
- 256GB storage variant dominates sales (5.1K units)
- Greece leads net revenue; Nigeria is weakest market
